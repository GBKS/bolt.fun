Improve your Bitcoin and Lighting Network knowledge by attending high-level lectures from world-class experts. Meet fellow bitcoiners to enrich your network and build together.

The role of ‘Lightning Halloween’ is to give space to the Lightning network community and development. It aims to improve the knowledge of LN developers by offering mentorship sessions and talks to inspire your projects in view of the ‘Legends of Lightning’ global tournament, and give you the opportunity to improve the technical and design features.

"LEGENDS OF LIGHTNING" HACKATHON

"Legends Of Lightning" is an international hackathon promoted by Bolt.Fun, which aims to bring together bitcoiners to build infrastructures and applications for the development of the Lightning Network adoption.

The application for the event will start on the 12th of October. The hackathon gives developers unlimited freedom to work on whatever they want, either alone or in teams. The deadline to submit your projects will be on the 30th of November.

The event's prize pool will be around ~$60,000 (3 BTC) in bitcoin and the winners will be announced on the 7th of December. The judging panel will consist of technologists, word class experts, industry leaders, and LN Venture Capitals.

For more information – link Legends of Lightning
